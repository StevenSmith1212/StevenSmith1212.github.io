Pitch

You are passengers on The Promethean, a luxury voyage vessel travelling from the Port of Barcelona to the Port of New Orleans. The upper deck hold the entertainment and main halls for the paid passengers of the ship, while the lower decks hold the storage, engine rooms, and other maitanence and utility areas. You are you on the ship. Passenger, worker, or someone else?

The Incident

The ship is 12 hours away from New Orleans Port. During character creation think about why you were in Barcelona, and why you are heading to New Orleans. Think about the people you are associated with and the people you might know on the ship. The adventure will start on the ship, and may take several sessions before getting to land.

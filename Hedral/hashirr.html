Edophian Peninsula

The Edophain Peninsula was given to the Aekolos government after their involvement in a scorching war The climate is wrongly thought to consist of only dry desert, by the geography of the peninsula ranges from a silvery wave of dunes to rich southern jungles to the northern Frost Edge Region.

Many people and cultures travel to the peninsula for travel, religious pilgrimage, or exploration. Many of those cultures have made a life for themselves in larger cities or have collected into communities around the small villages scattered across the region.

The land is further divieded into poltical regions:

The Edophain Block...

is the location of the capital city of Edophia, a metropolis of stirring culture and tradition, ruled as a Geniocracy.

Dwelyian Hallows...

is a region of religious significance, common are druidic rituals and surges of primal magics. Those who worship the land, sun, and sky travel here.

Silikian Coast. ..

is the home to the largest trading city, Medzul. A home for traders, pirates, those looking for adventure.

Eflynian Valley...

is a humble region home to few proud Edophian cultures. The good thing about the Eflynian Valley, is that nothing happens in the Eflynian Valley.

Torel Northlands...

Bleeding into the rest of Hashirr, this area contain colder mountainous ranges. Many Arcane Factions have stakes here. Perhaps for research, perhaps for something else.

Halfyn Kingdom...

the largest of the polical regions. Ruled by a Dwarven clans, but home to many. This kindom holds traditions and values related to the olde lands. Secrets that have rested for centuries.
